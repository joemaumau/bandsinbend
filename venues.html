Bands in Bend

Bend Music Venues

Every room in Bend where live music happens.

Silver Moon Brewing
Brewery · Music Venue

One of Bend's most active live music spots, Silver Moon hosts shows nearly every night of the week across multiple genres.

Hayden Homes Amphitheater
Outdoor Amphitheater

Bend's premier outdoor concert venue on the Deschutes River, hosting major touring acts from spring through fall.

Tower Theater
Historic Theater

A beautifully restored 1940s movie palace in downtown Bend, now one of Central Oregon's top performing arts venues.

Volcanic Theater Pub
Bar · Music Venue

An intimate venue with a great sound system and outdoor courtyard, hosting a strong mix of local and touring acts.

Domino Room
Music Venue · Bar

Connected to Midtown Ballroom, the Domino Room is a beloved Bend venue for indie, rock, and electronic acts.

The Commonwealth Pub
Bar · Live Music

A neighborhood pub with a consistent live music calendar featuring local acts and open mic nights.

Worthy Brewing
Brewery · Live Music

Bend's east side brewery with a spacious taproom and outdoor stage, regularly hosting live music on weekends.

Bevel Craft Brewing
Brewery · Live Music

A laid-back craft brewery in Bend with a rotating schedule of local musicians and an outdoor patio.

Rivers Place Taphouse
Taphouse · Live Music

A neighborhood taphouse with regular live music bookings in a relaxed, unpretentious setting.

Father Luke's Room
Bar · Live Music

An intimate bar with a warm atmosphere and regular live music from local Bend artists.

Northside Bar & Grill
Bar & Grill · Live Music

A neighborhood staple on Bend's north side with live music on weekends and a loyal local crowd.

Dogwood at the Pine Shed
Bar · Live Music

A newer addition to Bend's live music scene with late-night bookings and a lively atmosphere.

On Tap
Bar · Live Music

A casual Bend bar with a solid local music calendar and a welcoming neighborhood vibe.

M&J Tavern
Tavern · Live Music

One of Bend's classic neighborhood bars, hosting live music with a relaxed, unpretentious crowd.
